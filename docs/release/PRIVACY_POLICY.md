# Privacy Policy for OneLine

Effective Date: February 27, 2026

## 1. Overview

OneLine ("we", "us", "our", or "Application") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information in our mobile application.

## 2. Information Collection

### What We Do NOT Collect

OneLine does not collect, store, or transmit:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number, age, etc.)
- Location data
- Device identifiers or tracking information
- Analytics or usage statistics
- Cookies or tracking technologies
- Financial or payment information

### What We Store Locally

All gameplay data and preferences are stored exclusively on your device:
- Current level progress
- Completed levels list
- Sound and haptic settings
- Theme preferences (light/dark/system)

This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

## 3. User Data Security

Your privacy and security are important to us:
- All progress is stored locally on your device
- No network connectivity is required for gameplay
- No external API calls transmit user information
- You can clear all local data anytime via Settings > Clear App Data

## 4. Third-Party Services

OneLine uses minimal third-party dependencies:
- Flutter UI framework (open source)
- Provider for state management (open source)
- Hive for local storage (open source)

None of these libraries collect personal data.

## 5. Children's Privacy

OneLine does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The game is designed for all ages and contains no inappropriate content.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated "Effective Date" above.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
Email: support@oneline.app
Website: https://oneline.app

## 8. Compliance

- **GDPR Compliant:** We collect no personal data subject to GDPR
- **CCPA Compliant:** We collect no personal data covered by CCPA
- **Google Play Policy Compliant:** We respect Play Store privacy requirements

This application is 100% privacy-first. Your data stays with you.
