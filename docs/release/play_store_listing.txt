APP NAME:
OneLine

SHORT DESCRIPTION (max 80 characters):
Connect all dots in one continuous line without lifting your finger.

FULL DESCRIPTION (max 4000 characters):

OneLine is a minimalist puzzle game that challenges your problem-solving skills. Your goal is simple: connect all dots on the screen using a single continuous stroke without lifting your finger.

KEY FEATURES:

- Minimalist Design: Clean, distraction-free gameplay
- 30+ Carefully Crafted Levels: Progressive difficulty from simple to challenging
- Perfect Path Badge: Achieve a "Perfect" rating for clean solutions
- Sound & Haptic Feedback: Toggle audio and vibration as preferred
- Dark Mode Support: Play comfortably in any lighting
- Local Progress Saving: Your progress is saved automatically
- Privacy First: No data collection, no accounts required

HOW TO PLAY:

1. Look at the puzzle grid with dots scattered across the screen
2. Touch and draw a continuous line
3. Try to connect all dots without lifting your finger
4. If you lift before completion, you fail the level
5. Connect all dots in one pure stroke to advance

DIFFICULTY PROGRESSION:

Start with simple line patterns and work your way up to complex maze-like configurations. Each level tests your strategic thinking and hand coordination.

WHO SHOULD PLAY:

- Puzzle game enthusiasts
- Players seeking a focused, meditative experience
- Those who enjoy minimalist game design
- Anyone looking for a challenging mental exercise

PRIVACY:
